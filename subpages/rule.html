Rules

Offense

Main offensive sets to keep in mind.

  • 1) Can not pick up the ball and then continue dribbling
  • 2) When going for a lay up or any play you are allowed maximum of 2 steps without dribbling before you let go of ball
  • 3) When still you are allowed a pivot foot which can move while the other stays grounded

    • Defense

    Main Defensive sets to keep in mind

  • 1) no block ateempt or steal must make hard physical contact
  • 2) Blocking foul-when you interfere with the offesive players lane to hoop
  • 3) defensive players can move freely without any restrictions

    • General

    Main general rules to keep in mind.

    • 1) When grabbing rebounds make sure not to go over the back
    • 2) Shooter must be given landing space
    • 3) when posting up in the paint players must not flare out elbows
